# BYD Market Friction Audit

| Theme | Definition | Example Phrases | % Comments | Trend | Segments | Intensity |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------|-------|----------|-----------|
| Range Anxiety | Real-world range falls short of claimed specs | "420 km claimed but only 310 km real-world", "Not enough for highway trips" | 28% | ↓ Improving (early adopters report good experiences) | India 40%, Europe 35% | High |
| Charging Infrastructure | Lack of accessible public charging outside China | "No charging stations in my area", "Takes 2 hours at home" | 22% | → Stable | India 45%, Europe 50% | High |
| Reliability & Durability | Skepticism about long-term battery health | "Chinese batteries degrade fast", "No track record" | 19% | ↓ Declining (owners report good durability) | Europe 60%, older demographics | High |
| Price-to-Feature | Value vs Tesla / local competitors | "₹22L for this spec? Tesla is ₹25L", "Why pay premium?" | 18% | → Stable | India 50%, price-sensitive | Medium |
| Service & Warranty | After-sales support + parts availability concerns | "BYD has only 5 service centers", "Warranty claims rejected" | 13% | ↑ Improving (expansion ongoing) | Emerging markets 60% | High |
| Geopolitical Risk | China-India trade tensions, supply chain | "What if India bans Chinese EVs?", "US won't allow" | 9% | ↑ Increasing (post-2024 trade tensions) | Europe 70%, USA 80% | Medium |
| Brand Perception | Chinese origin seen as cheap/low-quality | "BYD is knockoff", "No prestige like Tesla" | 8% | ↓ Declining (awareness improving) | Europe 75%, premium segment | Low-Medium |

**Key Insights:**
- **Pragmatists (40% of market)** care most about: Service (35%), Range (30%), Reliability (25%)
- **Early Adopters (35%)** care about: Reliability (40%), Range (30%), but less skeptical
- **Skeptics (25%)** care about: Brand (60%), Geopolitics (70%), Reliability (50%)

**Recommendation:** Address Service + Range first (65% of market pain), then build brand trust for skeptics.
